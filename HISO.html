UNT HISO

Health Informatics Student Organization (HISO) is a student-led group that supports professional growth, networking, and learning opportunities in health informatics.

Mission

To help students build skills and confidence in health informatics through events, peer support, and connections with professionals in healthcare, data, and technology.

What We Do

  • Workshops and skill-building sessions (e.g., EHR, data analytics, informatics tools)
  • Guest talks with health informatics professionals
  • Networking and mentorship opportunities
  • Community-building and student support

Who Can Join

Open to all students interested in health informatics, healthcare data, and technology—beginners are welcome.
