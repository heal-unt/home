Health Informatics Student Organization (HISO)


HISO is a student-led organization that supports students interested in health informatics, healthcare data, and digital health technologies through professional development, networking, and hands-on learning opportunities.

Mission

To build a supportive community for students and help them grow academically and professionally in the field of health informatics.

What we do

  • Guest speaker sessions with health informatics professionals
  • Workshops on EHRs, data analytics, and informatics tools
  • Networking and mentorship opportunities
  • Student collaboration, events, and community building
