Prospective Students

  • The IDEA lab conducts research on the following area:
  • Information Quality, Data Quality Evaluation, Management and Quality Assurance of Big Data
  • Information Retrieval, Natural Language Processing, Data Mining, Deep learning
  • Intelligent Bioinformatics, Computational Law
  • Information Security and Privacy, Blockchain
  • Computer Science, Data Science, and Software Engineering Education
  • We welcome undergraduate and graduate students to the lab.

In your email, try to include the following information:

  • Use title as "Prospective Student: YourName - YourAffliation"
  • Briefly describe your (1) education background (anything you want to highlight); (2) research publications and experiences in areas related to NLP, ML and data mining; (3) programming skills.
  • Briefly talk about "why I'm (potentially) a good mentor to work with or to help your research?" and "what are the things you want to explore together with me?"
  • Include a PDF version of your CV.
