The Health Informatics Empowerment and Adaptation Lab (HEAL) at the University of North Texas is dedicated to advancing innovation in health informatics through cutting-edge research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and impactful applications. Our mission is to empower individuals, healthcare providers, and policymakers with data-driven insights and adaptable informatics solutions that address the evolving challenges of modern healthcare systems.

HEAL focuses on pioneering research in areas such as health data quality enhancement, medical concept normalization, trustworthy and equitable AI systems for healthcare, and the integration of large-scale health informatics platforms. By leveraging state-of-the-art technologies, including machine learning, natural language processing, and generative AI, we strive to improve decision-making, enhance patient outcomes, and promote health equity. The lab is committed to fostering collaboration across disciplines, working closely with healthcare professionals, academic researchers, and industry leaders. HEAL also provides opportunities for students and scholars to engage in transformative projects, preparing the next generation of leaders in health informatics.

At HEAL, we believe in harnessing the power of data to drive innovation, improve health services, and create meaningful impacts on local and global healthcare ecosystems.
